Rice and Beef Stew Recipe

Steps to follow to prepare a delicious bowl of rice and beef stew

If you're looking to dive into the kitchen, here is a dinner idea that will help you prepare something savoury that has a pleasant flavour

All you need is a list of fresh ingredients that you can grab at your nearest grocery store

Ingredients

  • Half a cup of rice
  • 250g of beef
  • Two onions, chopped
  • Three tomatoes, chopped
  • Three moderately sized carrots, chopped
  • One green capsicum, chopped
  • 3 cloves of Garlic, minced
  • 30g fresh Coriander leaves
  • One glass of water
  • Cooking oil

Instructions

Part 1: Cooking Rice

  1. Heat two glasses of water inside a pot until it boils
  2. Add the rice into the boiling water
  3. Let it cook for 20 minutes at medium heat
  4. Switch off the stove and move onto cooking the meat

Part 2: Cooking Meat

  1. Pour three tablespoons of oil in a separate pot
  2. Heat the oil for 3 minutes
  3. Add two chopped onions. Stir and let them cook until they turn translucent
  4. Add the minced garlic into the pot and let it cook for 2 minutes
  5. Add the minced meat and stir. Let it cook for 3 minutes
  6. Add a glass of water into the mixture and mix. Let it cook for 10 minutes
  7. Add the chopped carrots and capsicum and let them cook for another 15 minutes
  8. Add your tomatoes and salt then stir the mixture. Let it cook for 5 minutes
  9. Pour the coriander and stir. Let it cook for one minute
  10. Serve the spaghetti with the minced meat stew

ENJOY!
